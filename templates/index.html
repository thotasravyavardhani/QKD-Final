BB84 Protocol

Developed by Bennett and Brassard in 1984, BB84 is the first and most widely implemented quantum key distribution protocol, providing unconditional security based on quantum mechanics.

Quantum Advantage

Unlike classical cryptography, quantum key distribution offers information-theoretic security. Any eavesdropping attempt disturbs the quantum states, immediately alerting legitimate parties.
